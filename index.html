FACTS ABOUT THE EIFFEL TOWER

Every year, about 7 million people visit the place.
Every year the tower is painted with about 60 tons of paint.
In 1982 the elevators were replaced after 97 years of service, and the ascent time was reduced from 8 minutes to 2 minutes.
At night the tower is lit by 336 projectors.
